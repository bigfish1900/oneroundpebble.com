Sunday (16th) Update

Nov 17, 2008

We had a slightly better day today, James went back onto the drug again, just on a low dosage which made for an ok Sunday.

It's a bit of a juggling act at the moment. Because he spent so much time in ICU he got serious adictions to a couple of drugs and now, if he comes of them to quickly (we are down to the last one) it will cause him to slip back a bit with his condition (not feed so well and lose some of the weight he's starting to put on).

Hopefully the Pain Relief specialist will have another look this monday morning and come up with a new plan which will let us get out of hospital sometime soon.
