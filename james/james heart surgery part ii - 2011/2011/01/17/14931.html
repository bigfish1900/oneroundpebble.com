On the Ward

Jan 17, 2011

James was a really tired boy again today, slept quite a bit and was a bit grumpy (can't blame him for that though).

We did have a bit of fun in the afternoon though. The Nurse gave him some Medazolam. Basically, it zonks them out and makes them forget (although it can sometimes make kids a bit hypo).

It put James into a really good mood and he was nice and relaxed when they took out the drains in his chest.

He and Lyds had great fun playing with bubbles while the nurses did the work.

Afterwards he started to act like a drunk. Guess that was a bit of an insite to James in 20 years time [:P]
