Feeding

Nov 19, 2008

Today we had a couple of really good feeds. We are limited to how much we are allowed to give the little guy in a single feed and we have been alternating between the feeding tube and orally. Unfortunately we don't have a home date still, hopefully it will be discussed again over the next couple of days by the staff. We are still hopeful for the weekend, but not making plans.
