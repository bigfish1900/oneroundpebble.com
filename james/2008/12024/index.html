Saturday Update

Nov 2, 2008

Today was another step forward.

We had an awake little man this morning.

James finally got used to the seditives that have been used up till now. This meant that he was dosed up, but still trying to be awake.

The Nurses and Doctors are all happy with where he's at right now, there is still a long way to go, but we are making progress.

Lyds and I are both in that nervous state where we don't want to relax, but are really happy that things are moving forward.
