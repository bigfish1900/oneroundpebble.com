Sunday Update

Nov 3, 2008

The daughter of one of our friends today asked her mum if she can visit James when he no longer has a broken heart.

 

If James keeps going like he is a the moment, that will hopefully be soon.

 

The Doctors and Nurses are happy with his progress, and hoping to close his chest tomorrow (which will be brilliant).

 

The little guy is awake a fair bit of the time now, but not in pain and not moving to much (they put him to sleep when he gets to wriggly).

 

We are still doing one day at a time, but things are definitely looking better than this time last week.
