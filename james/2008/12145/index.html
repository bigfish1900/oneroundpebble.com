Going for a walk

Nov 12, 2008

Sorry I didn’t get this up earlier, fell asleep last night beside James’ bed rather than typing. While I was at work today James took Mum and Nanna for a walk. Given that all he wanted was a coffee scroll, he only took them down to the Cafe, but it was the first time he’d been outside for weeks. We also got moved to one of the lower care rooms on the ward (the nurse patient ratio is not quite as good, but the nurses are still just as good). Hopefully if the little guy can get his feeding sorted out and off a couple more of the oral drugs we can start talking about a go home date (not getting our hopes up, but there is only so much more we need to do before the hospital isn’t going to be doing much).
