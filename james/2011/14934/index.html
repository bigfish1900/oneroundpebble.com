One Week Later

Jan 26, 2011

So, it's been a week since we left the hospital.

James is going really well. If you didn't know that he'd had surgery less than two weeks ago, you'd never guess it unless you saw the scar.

He's not on any pain medication, not even Panadol. It's truly amazing how little kids recover.

The scar is healing really well.
