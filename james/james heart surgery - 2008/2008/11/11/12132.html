Big Boy Bed

Nov 11, 2008

Last night and this morning were not to much fun for the little guy but a few changes where made with have helped let James get off to sleep.

 

All the IV drugs where turned off, so no more Morphine which means no more Central line. As of tonight all we have left in is the feeding line into his nose and a couple of monitor that are stuck on his skin.

 

The stitches on his chest will come out on Wednesday.

 

As he’s regulating his own temperature properly he has been moved into a proper cot rather than the new borns heater bed which is really cool.
