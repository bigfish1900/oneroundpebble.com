Grumpy

Nov 16, 2008

Sorry for missing a day, I didn’t have much of an update and was too tired to do it last night (and totally forgot this morning).

 

Friday was a boring day, no much happened, but the decision was made to start weaning James off of another of the pain relief drugs.

 

That has made for a painful Saturday. From 8 this morning till 9 tonight, the longest “sleep” has been 10 minutes and most of the day he has been really restless.

 

Needless to say Lyds and I are both exhausted, but glad he got to see the doctor this evening to try and get something done so we don’t have such a bad day tomorrow.

 

The nurses are still talking about the possibility of going home sometime this week, hopefully this is the case, as hard as it’ll be, we all just want to go home.
