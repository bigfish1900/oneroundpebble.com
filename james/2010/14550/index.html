Going Back Again

Dec 1, 2010

Over the last 6 months or so, James' heart problems have become a little more serious again. He has a <A title="Subaortic Stenosis" href="http://www.rch.org.au/cardiology/health-info.cfm?doc_id=3535" target=_blank mce_href="http://www.rch.org.au/cardiology/health-info.cfm?doc_id=3535">Subaortic Stenosis that needs to be repaired sooner rather than later.

At the moment we are looking at next Monday for the Surgury, we have pre-admission tomorrow (Wednesday) so we can get the final scans and health check before we go in next week.

At the moment we don't know what is going to be done in the surgury, one of the meetings tomorrow is to find out exactly what the surgeon has planned and what sort of recovery times they are hoping for. From what I've read, the average for this sort of work is around a week in hospital and then the normal follow up visits, so hopefully we'll be well and truly home for Christmas.

I'm just trying to figure out the Internet Access while we are in hospital, but I'm planning on using this blog again to give updates on what's happening while we are in there.
