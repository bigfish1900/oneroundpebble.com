Another Week

Nov 22, 2008

The Cardio Doctors are not really happy with how James' stridor (the Paralysed Vocal Cords) is progressing.

It the main thing holding us back now. Because the Vocal Cords on the left are not moving, it means that the little guy is having problems with his feeding (it also makes his breathing sound dreadful at times).

Given this, we where told today that there will probably be another week before we get to go home. Which is a real bummer. We are all exhausted and well ready to go back home, but we would rather have him fixed before we go home rather than having another rush back.
