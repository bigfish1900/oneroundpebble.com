Roll over

Jan 6, 2009

Lyds got a bit of a fright yesterday. She lay James down on his lambs wool blanket and left him playing with some toys while she did a few dishes (the little guy goes through a lot of bottles every day). When Lyds came back a couple of minutes later, he was on his front having rolled himself over.

With the notable exception of first nappy to leak, I think that Lyds have been around for most of the firsts so far (first smile, etc).

He is so much more alert than when we left the hospital last month, and continues to put on weight.

I'm expecting a grumpy man tonight, he's having another Immunisation today, so he probably wont be too happy for the next couple of days. [:(]
